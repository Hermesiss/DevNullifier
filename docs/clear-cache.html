How to Clear Development Caches Safely

Development caches can consume gigabytes of disk space over time. Learn how to safely clear them without breaking your projects.


From Node.js modules to Unity caches, we'll show you the safest methods to reclaim disk space while preserving your development workflow.

Important Warnings

Never delete: Source code, .git folders, or active project files.
Be careful with: Global package caches that may break other projects.
Always backup: Important projects before cleaning.
